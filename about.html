I am a sound designer with a focus on game and film. During my recent education between the University of Sussex and Rensellaer Polythenic Institute, I have created a collection of projects to learn more indepth techniques and skills to guide myself to my goals.

During this time I have focused on creating games whith heavy focuses on sound, both alone (The Woods) but also in a small team (Journey Away). I also aim to create intuative and open source tools to make work around soundscapes easier. With my eight channel mixer, people can use their phones or tables to pan and effect sound in an array while being mobile within the space.
